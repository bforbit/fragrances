The world of fragrance has allured me for years. It's one of the greatest forms of self-expression. I've been fortunate to have tested a wide range of scents from major brands, from beautiful full-sized bottles to mini samples that help me refine my preferences. With all the vials and bottles in my stock, I assembled a spreadsheet of names and notes as I use them, and I'm thrilled to share with you my perfume library in the form of this interactive webpage.

Choose one of the many notes below and click "Get Fragrances." You might find your next scent! The list will grow over time and eventually develop into a more customized experience.
